Fun Pet Facts

💓

Pets Boost Your Mood

Petting a dog or cat for just a few minutes lowers cortisol levels and boosts serotonin — nature's own stress-relief toolkit.

👃

A Dog's Nose Is Unique

Every dog has a unique nose print — just like human fingerprints. Their sense of smell is up to 100,000 times sharper than ours!

😴

Cats Are Champion Sleepers

Cats sleep 12–16 hours a day on average. They're crepuscular (most active at dawn and dusk), saving energy for bursts of play.
