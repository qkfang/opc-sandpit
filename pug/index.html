Fun Pug Facts

  • Pugs are famous for their curly tails, expressive eyes, and wonderfully wrinkly faces.
  • Because of their short snouts, pugs can be delightfully snorty and legendary little snorers.
  • Pugs trace their origins back to ancient China, where they were treasured companion dogs.
